Dam House ££
Perfect For: Summer
Located in Crookes Valley Park, Dam House offers a beautiful view over the whole park and is perfect for a day out with the family. They serve a range of food including pizzas, as well as great value drinks.

Hallamshire House £
Perfect For: Drinks with friends
A favourite pub in the heart of Crookesmoor, the super comfy sofas of Hallamshire House along with the wide selection of locally brewed craft beers create a warm and friendly atmosphere. Be sure to check out the heated beer garden for those colder evenings!

Frog and Parrot ££
Perfect For: Sunday Roasts
The Frog and Parrot on Division Street has a welcoming atmosphere, offering great deals on a range of drinks. The quirky decor and great food makes this pub a great all rounder, good for all occasions!

Fox and Duck £
Perfect For: Trying some local brews
An old punters vibe with a university socials crowd, Fox and Duck is in the heart of Broomhill and has a legendary selection of local ales on tap- see if you can complete the challenge!

Nottingham House £
Perfect for: winter warmer food for you and the whole flat
Well known lagers, local ales, pool tables and most importantly- pies, mash and peas for £7.95! Nottingham House serves up great food and drink and plays a tonne of good music, a solid Pint Finder suggestion!

The Cavendish ££
Perfect for: a bite to eat in between lectures
Located in the heart of West Street, this student-orientated take on Wetherspoons as an epic menu selection for budget prices and has a buzzing array of events every month. From open mic nights to bingo to Clash of the Cans pub quiz, this is a must do for students!

Bungalow and Bears ££
Perfect for: a chilled night out with live music
Bang in the middle of Division Street this bar boasts great character in its old fire station set up and plays a regular host to live bands, resident DJ’s and a mash up of craft beer and cocktails.

The Harley ££
Perfect for: a burger and beer after a long library sesh!
When it isn’t full to the brim with Sheffield students dancing the night away, The Harley is vibrant and artsy pub that serves up a great variety of craft beer and local eats from Twisted Burger Company.

The Wick at Both Ends £££
Perfect for: trying something new
Fairy lights for ambience and an endlessly creative cocktail menu for your taste buds, this West Street gem provides delicious bites during the day and DJ delights though till 2am to keep the fun burning!
